Plastic Free Challenge

Building a cleaner planet through zero-waste movements that change small daily habits.

Project Introduction

Plastic Free Challenge is a youth-led environmental protection campaign to reduce disposable plastic use in daily life and spread a sustainable consumption culture. We act with the belief that small actions together make big changes.

Key Activities

  • • Campaign for tumbler use in local cafes
  • • Host zero-waste pop-up stores and workshops
  • • Propose and collaborate with companies for plastic alternatives
  • • Social media relay challenges to improve public awareness
