About the Project

Every weekend, high school students gather for plogging (picking up trash while jogging) in urban areas. We don't just pick up trash; we record the types and locations of the waste we collect to create an 'Environmental Vulnerability Map' for our neighborhood.

Key Activities

  • Plogging every Saturday at 10 AM
  • Waste data classification and digital mapping
  • Correct waste separation campaigns for residents
  • Publishing annual environmental maps and proposing policies
