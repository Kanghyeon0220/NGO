Urban Green Campaign

March - August 2025 | Environmental Project

Project Overview

The 6-month 'Urban Green Campaign' transformed abandoned urban lots into recreational spaces for residents and ecological habitats for wildlife. Every week, volunteers collected waste, planted trees, and created ecological parks.

Key Activities

  • Regular plogging sessions every Saturday (24 total)
  • Waste classification and recycling
  • Tree planting events with local residents (3 events)
  • Ecological park creation: benches, walkways, flower beds
  • Environmental education campaigns

Participant Testimonials

Minsu Kim (University Student): "It was rewarding to see the space change every week. Amazing how an abandoned lot became this beautiful."

Jieun Lee (Local Resident): "Now we have a clean space for our kids. Thank you to all who participated."

Future Plans

Building on this success, we plan to expand in 2026 to more urban spaces, creating community-driven ecological parks. Our efforts for sustainable urban environments continue.
