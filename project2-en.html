The Dreaming Yellow Classroom

Sharing the joy of coding with underprivileged children.

About the Project

This is a high school mentoring program for elementary students who have limited opportunities for digital education due to economic or regional factors. We focus on creative and logical thinking through hands-on activities like block coding and robot kits rather than complex theories.

Key Activities

  • Bi-weekly Saturday coding classes at local children centers
  • Distribution of custom-made coding textbooks
  • Simple website building and art-integrated coding projects
  • Supporting the annual 'Junior Yellow Hackathon'
