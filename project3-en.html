Plastic Free Challenge

Small daily actions add up to create massive change.

About the Project

This is an online/offline hybrid campaign planned by high school students to tackle environmental pollution caused by reckless plastic use. We spread a culture of using reusable containers instead of disposables, and collaborate with local cafes to operate our 'Bring Your Own Cup' program.

Key Activities

  • Online Plastic Free certification challenge (SNS)
  • Local cafe partnership: discount on lemonade when using a tumbler
  • Campaign promoting alternative straws instead of plastic straws
  • Distribution of our own 'Zero Waste Starter Kit'
